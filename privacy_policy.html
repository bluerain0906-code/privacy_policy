7. Special Notes on Location Data

Location data is essential to the core functionality of this Application. Please pay special attention to the following:

7.1 Foreground Location

When you open this Application and start a task, we will obtain your real-time location through the device's GPS or network positioning for route recording and trajectory tracking.

7.2 Background Location

If you enable "Allow Background Location" in the settings, this Application will continue to obtain your location information in the background. This feature is used for:

  • Continuous trajectory recording during an ongoing task
  • Safety monitoring (detecting abnormal situations such as prolonged stationary status)
  • Sharing your real-time location with team members

You may disable background location at any time in the settings. Once disabled, the Application will be unable to record trajectories or perform safety monitoring while in the background.

7.3 Smart Tracking Strategy

In team mode, this Application automatically adjusts its positioning strategy based on your device status:

  • Charging with stable network: Primarily uses network positioning, with GPS calibration every 1 minute for trajectory accuracy
  • Not charging with stable network: Primarily uses network positioning, with GPS calibration every 5 minutes for trajectory accuracy
  • No network signal: Automatically switches to continuous GPS positioning
  • Network restored: Automatically switches back to network positioning, with GPS resuming intermittent calibration

8. Special Notes on Health Data

If you choose to connect a wearable device (such as Apple Watch) and enable the heart rate detection feature, this Application will read your heart rate data. This data is used solely for safety monitoring purposes, including:

  • Detecting abnormal heart rate (below 45 bpm or above 140 bpm)
  • Serving as a reference for backend safety decision logic
  • Conducting comprehensive risk assessment in combination with other indicators (such as stationary duration, battery level, fall detection)

Heart rate data will not be shared with any third party, nor will it be used for advertising or marketing purposes. You may disable the heart rate detection feature at any time in the settings.
